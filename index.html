Skyscraper Ragdoll Run

A third-person physics playground with a very high tower.

Walk inside and climb the interior stairs, or step onto the floor teleport pad to go straight to the top.

Hold right mouse and drag for free camera control. Use Space to jump.

When ready, press F to turn your humanoid into a full ragdoll and watch the fall.
